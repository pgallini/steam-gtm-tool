About This Game

COLLECT, SUMMON, DOMINATE

Creepy Horrors: My Little Blood Cult 2 combines classic video game monster-collecting with loot-driven progression in a darkly toned turn-based battle system. Conduct demonic rituals with blood and alchemical materials to summon and bind Creepy Horrors to your will!

Collect them all to complete your curio cabinet. Venture into crypts to explore their secrets with your six horrors, and defend yourself in 3 v 3 battles to the death. Issue orders for attacks, special abilities, defensive maneuvers, and item use to raise your cult to glory.

A FRIENDLY, SACRIFICIAL BLOOD DRIVE!

It’s not a satanic blood sacrifice, it’s a familial blood drive! Find your forever family by joining a cult: choose from Secta Daemonum (Demon), Secta Mortuorum (Undead), Secta Spirituum (Spirit) and Secta Cryptidarum (Cryptid) in the decaying town of Hunter’s Haven.

Collect blood offerings by lovingly executing consenting cult followers, combining their lifeblood with alchemical materials to summon Creepy Horrors to battle. Each cult unlocks its own unique breed of merciless creatures to summon, train, upgrade and unleash on your less equipped neighbors.

SOME CALL IT A CULT. YOU CALL IT HOME.

Summon Horrors from the Beyond and bind them to your will! Collect them all to complete your collection in an unlimited at-home roster, and select a team of six to accompany you into battle.

Perform a ritual discovered by exploring crypts or killing enough of a certain Horror to reverse engineer its materials.

Is your favorite Creepy Horror just a bit too weak for your plans of domination? Dismember it for parts! Ligaments and eyeballs and creepy crawly parts alike can be used to upgrade other Horrors into bigger, better, fueled monstrosities.

CONQUER RIVAL CULTS

Choose carefully! Each cult controls a region with its own combat flavor.

  • Secta Daemonum: Call on the power of the Hells to inflict brutal damage on your enemies

  • Secta Mortuorum: Defy death itself and wield an army of the undead, capable of surviving nearly any blow

  • Secta Spirituum: Manipulate the power of souls to strengthen your armies and weaken your enemies

  • Secta Cryptidarum: Nimbly evades attacks and embrace a chaotic, unpredictable offense

Build a horrific fever dream team of six. Next, customize your party’s build with relic charms that alter stats, unlock abilities, and create devastating synergies and buffs.

Enter merciless 3 v 3 battle and strategically recall and deploy Horrors during your turn to boost your strengths or adapt to your opponent’s weaknesses.

YOUR way is the ONLY way to live. So rise in rank and dominate over the other cults prove your ideals!

What Lies Below?

What brought you here? What can you uncover with grotesque monster guts and pools of blood? Foster your devotion...

DARK, CHARMING, AND UTTERLY ADDICTIVE

Creepy Horrors: My Little Blood Cult 2 commits fully to the cute-but-horrific energy of its predecessor, My Little Blood Cult! Whereas the original My Little Blood Cult focuses on fishing for horrific demons and showcasing an addictive collection, Creepy Horrors expands into the RPG world of a 3 v 3 creature battler while upgrading the creature-collector experience.

Each Creepy Horror is a hand-drawn, fun-cute nightmare made by a small team. We’re in love with marrying sinister mechanics with dark humor and making fantasy bloodshed feel like a good time.

KEY FEATURES

  • Four Cults, Four Religions: Pledge to Demon, Spirit, Cryptid, or Undead, each with its own breed of merciless creatures and combat flavor.

  • Sacrificial Blood Offerings: Lovingly execute your most devoted followers and harvest their lifeblood for the cause.

  • Creature Collection: Summon, train, and dismember dozens of hand-drawn Horrors to build your perfect nightmare roster.

  • Deep Customization: Equip relic charms to upgrade stats, unlock abilities, and forge devastating synergies worthy of the Beyond.

  • Tactical 3 v 3 Combat: Command devastating attacks, deploy sinister abilities, and orchestrate a nightmare on the battlefield

  • Strategic Swapping: Recall and deploy Horrors mid-battle to exploit weaknesses or save your favorites from an untimely second death.

  • Boss Battles: Rise in rank and face rival cult leaders in intense multi-phase showdowns.

  • RPG Progression: Collect eyeballs, ligaments, and creepy crawly parts to fuel upgrades between runs. Nothing goes to waste.

  • Dark Humor Meets Darker Rituals: Cute art, sinister mechanics, and the signature tone that made My Little Blood Cult a blood-soaked good time.
